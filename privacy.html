Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

Quwwaa LLC (“QUWWAA”) respects your privacy. This explains what we collect and why. The free, logged-out experience works without an account and without us collecting personal details.

What we collect

  • Account & profile (members): your name, email, the address style and interests you choose, and your city/state/country — so QUWWAA can personalize your board, brief, and assistant.
  • Subscription status: your plan state and identifiers needed to manage billing.
  • Assistant messages: what you type or say to the butler is sent to our AI providers to generate a reply. Voice is transcribed to text to answer you.

What we don’t collect

We never see or store your full payment card details — card data is handled directly by Stripe. News results are fetched live and are not tied to your identity.

Service providers

  • Supabase — secure account, authentication, and profile storage.
  • Stripe — subscription billing and card processing.
  • Anthropic (assistant replies / summaries) and OpenAI (voice transcription & spoken replies) — your messages are processed to produce responses.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms; we share only what’s needed.

Your choices

You can edit your profile in Account settings, cancel your subscription anytime via the Stripe portal, and request deletion of your account data by emailing us. Deleting your account removes your profile.

Email

If you opt into our newsletter or daily brief we’ll email you; every email includes an unsubscribe link.

Contact

Privacy questions? Email quwwaa.io@gmail.com.
